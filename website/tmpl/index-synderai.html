Synthetic Data Examples – Realistic – using AI

SYNDERAI provides realistic, privacy-preserving synthetic European healthcare data, including the first EU-Lab FHIR synthetic datasets. Explore reusable examples built with AI for interoperability and secondary use in healthcare systems.

VIEW ON GITHUB

Lab Reports were the first synthetic examples

First batch of synthetic examples were generated in October 2024 for the HL7 Europe FHIR Laboratory Report for the EHDS.

Clinically realistic Examples supported by AI

We use AI to support realistic clinical data scenarios with proper interdependencies.

Privacy & Anonymity

No real patient data; ensures privacy while enabling secondary use and testing.

Open-Source and Extensible

SYNDERAI is maintained under AGPL-3.0 and hosted on GitHub by HL7 Europe, with open principles, evolving examples, and a collaborative roadmap for future FHIR resources like Discharge Reports, Patient Summaries, and more.
