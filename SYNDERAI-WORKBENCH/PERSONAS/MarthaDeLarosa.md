**Martha DeLarosa**

Martha DeLarosa, a 53-year-old Dutch woman, experiences frequent menopausal flushes that disrupt her daily comfort and sleep quality.

She has been diagnosed as pre-diabetic type 2 and is actively working with a dietitian to adjust her nutrition and increase daily physical activity.

Martha takes oral anastrozole 1mg daily as part of her ongoing breast cancer remission care plan.

Due to a confirmed allergy to penicillins, her care providers maintain a strict medication safety protocol to prevent adverse reactions.

She finds support and motivation in a local women's health group, where she shares her journey and learns practical tips for managing hormonal changes and metabolic health.
